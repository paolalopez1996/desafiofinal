CONNOR MANSON

LEAD VOCALS, GUITAR, FRONT MUM.

"Always tried to use my voice in interesting ways, like an instrument, just experimenting with it is part of our songwriting."

Influences: "Jeff Buckley is my main influence, and I guess we have a similar range. I also grew up with lots of jazz and blues, which I still love massively. And there’s classic rock, too."

Age: Born on 26th December 1992, 29 years young

JOE LANGRIDGE-BROWN

GUITAR, BACKING VOCALS, KEYS, LYRICS

"I once met Felix Baumgartner and got starstruck. It doesn’t usually happen but the man went to space!"

Influences: " I think it must go back to what my Dad would play. We’d listen to Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers in the car, some Dylan and some U2. A lot of artists where songs sound like instant classics. Later on, I got the Foo Fighters album One By One and Radiohead The Bends. Those albums made me pick up a guitar and pretty much changed my life."

Age: Born on 29th November 1990

DOM CRAIK

GUITAR, KEYBOARD, PIANO, BACKING VOCALS

Influences: " We all started off with rock, right? Bands like Led Zeppelin, AC/DC, that sort of thing. More recently, I’ve been inspired by Arcade Fire, Radiohead, Jeff Buckley to an extent. And I’m also into dance music and producing, so that finds its way into what we do too."

Age: Born on 22nd August 1993
